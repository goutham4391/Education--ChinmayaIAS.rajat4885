Why Study For IAS

Civil Services give you a platform to serve the society and the nation with great emoluments and very high respectability in the society. You will also get additional benefits like good accommodation, official vehicle etc.

If you take steps to use your power with all the sincerity as an IAS, IPS, IFS or any other Civil Service Officer in the right way, you will always be remembered for your crucial role in shaping the countrys future. Eg: Dr. Kiran Bedi, Mr. T.N. Seshan, Ms. Nirupama Rao, Mr. Narendra Kumar, Mr. Ajit Doval.

You may get an opportunity to represent India on international platforms like United Nations and World Trade Organization.

If you would like to learn more, you will get 2 years of study leave and even get the opportunity to study at reputed foreign universities.
