Mock Test For TNPSC Exams: Why You Need Them

Do you remember all those tests and revision exams you wrote just before appearing for your 10th and 12th board exams? Do you recall how these mock tests helped you face the real exam on D Day?

That’s exactly why mock tests are popular even when you go on to writing your competitive exams like TNPSC. Whether online or as part of TNPSC coaching classes, a mock test is part of your preparation package for competitive exams.

Mistakes made during a mock test provide valuable feedback to rectify and refine your preparation for the actual exam. Knowing your strengths and weaknesses helps you plan more efficiently to prepare for your examt.

As you continue reading our blog, you will understand the importance of taking mock tests and how they help you in your TNPSC journey.

Mock Tests: A Test-Based Learning Approach For TNPSC

The Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission conducts exams to select eligible candidates to work for the Tamil Nadu State Government every year. Popularly known as the TNPSC exams, it opens the doors to new jobs and livelihoods for thousands of aspirants.

Government jobs are always held in high esteem, despite the thousands of opportunities in the private sector. To get placed in a well-salaried government posting is a dream come true for many youngsters. It is one of the main reasons that drive the competition for TNPSC exams.

If you are a TNPSC aspirant, you need to arm yourself to beat the competition and ace the exams. One sure-fire way to do it is to take online mock tests, not just randomly, but every test that comes your way.

TNPSC online mock tests are treasure troves filled with previous TNPSC question papers a based on the TNPSC syllabus of various groups.

A mock test is a perfect solution to understand how these competitive exams work. It is an ideal test-based approach that gives you a practical perspective of the real exam. In a mock test, you:

  • Write the exam and interpret the results
  • Assess your strengths and weaknesses
  • Pinpoint areas of improvisation
  • Understand your level of preparedness
  • Analyse your accuracy levels

Attempting mock tests regularly allows you to level up your preparation. For first-timers, it gives them an in-depth perspective of the real exam, which is beneficial, especially for those who are prone to exam jitters.

Experts, however, suggest that mock tests are useful ONLY if they are used to the maximum. Do not miss the chance to take mock tests if your coaching centre offers them every other day. Every mock test refines your overall preparedness and gives you an extra advantage on the day of exams.

There are more benefits of taking mock tests or “test series”, as they are known in TNPSC exams.

6 Advantages of Taking Test Series For TNPSC Exams

1. Time management

In any exam, time is the biggest element. It can become your friend or your enemy, depending on how well you master the art of using it. The duration for each TNPSC paper is 3 hours, and you cannot afford to waste even a single moment.

Mock test series help you understand and strategise time management and complete the given questions within the stipulated time.

2. Tracking development

How will you know if you are preparing well or not? In a competitive exam like TNPSC, it would be foolish to remain ignorant about your preparedness levels. Mock tests are the best parameter to measure your development because they will reveal the truth through your scores.

Mock tests are based on:

  • Original scoring system
  • Original question pattern
  • Complexity of the exams

Therefore, you will know where exactly you stand based on your score.

3. Step-by-step improvement

Like every competitive exam, the TNPSC syllabus is vast and requires a consistent, well-analysed approach. Mock exams will cover the basics, the advanced applications, and the tricks to improve writing speed. This step-by-step focus will help enhance continuity to implement the same during the real exam.

4. Best practices

The mock test series teach you to adopt best practices that align with your preparation mode. You may prefer commencing with the challenging topics or thinking about dealing with what’s easy. Whatever your choice, you can take up tests after preparing for each topic and subject. These best practices will help you understand the real scenario and gear up for it.

5. In-depth familiarisation

Mock tests take you through each step of the preparation process. Therefore, you will encounter the challenges first-hand, familiarise yourself, and find the solution to deal with them. Without mock test series, these challenges will catch you unawares and derail your mindset when facing the real exam.

6. Consistent progress

The test series prepares the candidates both educationally and emotionally to reach their desired goals. Some candidates might have difficulty using modern technology, virtual processes, and applications. The test series can assist them in overcoming the difficulties and making themselves more comfortable with all the aspects.

Now that you know the benefits of taking test series for TNPSC preparation, it is also important to know how to use them to your advantage. We leave you with some proven ideas and tips to make the most of the test series and ace the exams with flying colours.

Tips To Optimise TNPSC Test Series For Your BenefitÂ 

  • Approach the exams with a confident and positive mindset.
  • Rely on self-motivation and inner strength to confront your challenges.
  • Get rid of exam anxiety and maintain a cool and calm exterior.
  • Learn to plan your time, improve accuracy, and boost your speed.
  • Complete the test paper at least five or ten minutes before the stipulated time.
  • Strategise your exam approach based on your strengths and weaknesses.
  • Prepare yourself mentally and physically to do a lot of hard work.

Here are some expert tips on refining your TNPSC exam preparation:

  • Read the question paper in a calm and composed manner.
  • Avoid attempting questions you are unsure of.
  • Clarify all your doubts in advance
  • Revise your topics every single day.
  • Give equal importance to each subject.
  • Divide your preparation schedule into slots based on difficulty levels.
  • Be well-prepared if you are taking online exams.
  • Ensure you get proper rest and sleep.
