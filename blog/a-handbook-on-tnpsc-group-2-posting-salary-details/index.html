General

TNPSC Group 2 is an examination conducted after the Group 1 exam as a second level by the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission. It is conducted offline, followed by an interview process for some posts, and the final document verification. Post examination, candidates who get selected in the interview process can become gazetted officers in the state government of Tamil Nadu. However, they have to work under Group 1 officers. Statistics state that over 1500 non-interview and interview CCSE-II vacancies are open for 2022. As a result, many candidates seek the help of a reputed online TNPSC coaching institute to score better. Let us discuss the various TNPSC Group 2 job postings and salary details for 2022 in detail.

An Overview on Job Postings for TNPSC Group 2

The TNPSC Group 2 examination conducted by Tamil Nadu’s Public Service Commission selects graduation for various fields across the state department. 5,529 vacancies are available for TNPSC Group 2 as per the 2022 annual calendar. The salary structure for every position for TNPSC Group 2A and Group 2 is mentioned below.

What Is the Eligibility Criteria for TNPSC Group 2?

TNPSC Group 2 was released on the 23rd of February in 2022 and it included the following points for eligibility:

Educational Qualification for TNPSC Group 2

The posts under the TNPSC Group 2 require the candidates to possess an under graduation or an equivalent from any recognized institution or university. A few additional educational qualifications are preferred for particular posts, which are stated in detail in the TNPSC Group 2 Recruitment 2022’s official notification.

Age Limits for TNPSC Group 2

The age limit is the same for both non-interview and interview postings. It will be calculated for the 1st of July 2022.
Sub-Registrar Grade-II – (20-30) years
Probation Officer in the Social Defence Department – (26-40) years
Probation Officer in the Prison Department – (22-30) years
For all posts (except Sub-Registrar Grade – II, Probation Officer in the Prison Department and Social Defence Department) – (18-30) years

Age relaxation is provided for differently-abled people is up to ten years beyond the maximum possible age limit. Though the age limit is forty-eight years for ex-servicemen, the age concession is not applicable in the case of applicants who are already been recruited to any other service previously.

Details on Salary Structure for TNPSC Group 2

The salary for TNPSC Group 2 is calculated based on the vacancies by the Tamil Nadu Public Service. Besides the basic pay, employees in Group 2 will be receiving benefits and perks. The total salary will be a sum of the basic pay, grade pay, dearness allowances, and other allowances. The take-home salary is deducted from tax, and the salary for TNPSC Group 2 is always calculated as per the 7th pay commission.

Allowances & Promotions

There are various allowances for employees of the State Government. Applicable perks are specified in every salary slip, and employees working in government organizations can get various services free of cost. A few common allowances are travel, medical, house rent, telephone, internet, pension, study leaves, and dearness allowance. Government employees get a salary hike with every promotion depending on their performance and skill. The promotion processes are slow, but the benefits are worth the wait.
